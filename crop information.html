Crop Information

Wheat

Wheat is mostly used to make rotis in India. It is also an important ingredient for making bread

  • Energy: 225 calories (per kg)
  • Protein: 13 g (per 100kg)
  • Calcium: 8 mg(per 100 g)
Rice

Rice is the seed of the grass species Oryza glaberrima or Oryza sativa. As a cereal grain, it is the most widely consumed staple food

  • Energy: 134 calories (per kg)
  • Protein: 16 g (per 100kg)
  • Calcium: 18 mg(per 100 g)
Millet

Millets are a group of highly variable small-seeded grasses, widely grown around the world as cereal crops or grains for fodder and human food.

  • Energy: 378.1 Calories (per 100 g)
  • Protein: 11 g (per 100 g)
  • Calcium: 8 mg (per 100 g)
